Announcing AIxB
June 19th 2023

Today we announce the formation of AIxB.

The goal of AIxB is to demystify the hidden secrets of the genomes.
We will share more information over the next couple of weeks and months.

Team

Our team is led by Ratnesh Kumar Sharma, Founder and CEO of AIxB. Ratnesh brings to the table not only his innovative ideas, but also an unwavering dedication to making a mark in the realm of biotechnology and AI.

We have previously worked at Amazon, Huawei, LLaMA, Front Row, Imperial College, London, IISc Bangalore, and the Kusuma School of Biological Sciences.

Together, we work passionately and tirelessly, with the shared goal of revolutionizing biotechnology and AI, pushing boundaries, and transforming the future.
